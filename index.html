PikaDish

Your Weekly Meal Planner

PikaDish is a personal project that I undertook with 3 other collegues. It is a mobile and web application that allows you to plan every meal you wish to cook in the given week, and then tally's a list of the required ingredients to cook these meals. As you can imagine, this is very helpful when planning your weekly shop.

You can view my website here.

DEValicious

Party Planner

Devalacious is a meal ordering app for parties at EDA. It allows everyone at the party to click on a meal and then displays the meal in a seperate list, and totals the amount of each meal. It was created by a team of four people in two days.

I came up with the idea of this application, when I attended my first graduation party and realised that the meal ordering process took roughly a hour due to the large number of meals, and no clear way to record every meal. It also gave me a excellent oppurtunity to hone my Angular JS knowledge.
